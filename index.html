What is a biokineticist?

A biokineticist is a healthcare professional who specializes in exercise therapy and rehabilitation. They assess, prescribe, and supervise exercise programs tailored to individual needs to improve physical function and overall well-being.

Slide 2

When should I see a Biokineticist?

  • If you suffer from a chronic condition that can be improved and maintained by regular exercise,
  • If you wish to start on a new exercise regime and do not know where to start,
  • If you would like to get guidance on making healthy lifestyle changes.

Slide 4

What conditions can a biokineticist treat?

Biokineticists can help manage a variety of conditions, including musculoskeletal injuries, chronic pain, post-operative rehabilitation, sports injuries, chronic diseases like diabetes and hypertension, and general fitness and wellness goals.

Slide 5

How does a session with a biokineticist typically go?

During a session, the biokineticist will conduct an initial assessment to evaluate your medical history, current fitness level, and specific goals. They will then design a personalized exercise program and provide guidance on proper technique and progression. Follow-up sessions may involve monitoring progress, adjusting the program as needed, and providing education on injury prevention and self-management strategies.
